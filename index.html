About Spider-Man

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a superhero created by writer Stan Lee and writer-artist Steve Ditko for Marvel Comics. First appearing in "Amazing Fantasy" #15 in 1962, Spider-Man is one of the most iconic superheroes of all time.
